Our Electric Vehicle Charging Program

Home Charging Station Rebates

Apply for $750 through Gulf Power's Electric Vehicle Charging Program after the purchase or lease of a qualifying Plug-In Electric vehicle and help offset some of the costs associated with installing a charging station in your home.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Offer is available to all Gulf Power residential customers who purchase or lease a qualifying plug-in electric vehicle.
  • Eligible vehicles include new or pre-owned, highway capable Battery Electric (BEV), Plug-in Hybrid
  • Electric (PHEV), and Extended Range Electric (EREV) vehicles. Low speed electric vehicles (top speed of 35 mph) are not eligible for this incentive.
  • An itemized paid sales receipt must be submitted as proof of purchase.
  • A completed incentive application form must be submitted no more than 60 days after purchase or lease of qualifying vehicle.
  • This program is limited to the first 1,000 participants and expires on December 31, 2018.
