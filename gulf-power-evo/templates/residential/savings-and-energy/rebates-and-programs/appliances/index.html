Earn Rebates When You Upgrade Your Appliances

You rely on your appliances to keep your household running smoothly. Today's energy-efficient home appliances do everything you need while using less energy and less water, which helps you save money.

If you're looking to upgrade, make sure to look for ENERGY STAR appliances. When it comes to saving energy and money, as well as durability, ENERGY STAR certified appliances are the best out there. And you might also be eligible for a rebate.

Look for blue label to make sure you're getting an ENERGY STAR certified appliance. Learn more at energystar.gov.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Offer is available to Gulf Power residential customers only.
  • Offer is limited to one of each type of appliance per customer address per calendar year.
  • Qualifying products must be new, operational, and must be purchased and installed during the effective dates of the program.
  • Proof of purchase and product qualifications (including manufacturer and the appropriate efficiency specifications of the products) is required for payment of incentive.
  • Application must be made within 60 days of the purchase of qualifying products.
