Power surges happen all the time, whether you are aware of them or not. Just one lightning-induced power surge can permanently damage your office equipment and appliances, and could cost your business:

  • Missed sales
  • Expensive damage to electric motor-driven equipment
  • Employee downtime
  • Lost perishables
  • Customer inconvenience

Our commercial surge protection solution is an easy, affordable way to protect your business from this very real threat. Keep your doors open and your sales and customer satisfaction high.

How It Works

Our commercial surge solution includes a device that creates a defense between powerful lightning-induced and related surges and your electric motor-driven equipment or resistance heating appliances.

The device is manufactured by TESCO, a leader in heavy-duty surge protection. The device does not limit normal electrical flow, but it does provide a clamping, or limiting, action in case of a voltage surge preventing damage to critical equipment such as:

  • HVAC compressors
  • Refrigerator and freezer compressors
  • Cooking equipment
  • Gasoline pump motors
  • Healthcare-related equipment and more

Note: This solution is designed exclusively to protect electrical motor-driven equipment, resistance heating elements and the electronic circuit boards associated with the specific piece of equipment. To further safeguard your sensitive electronics, such as computers and copiers, be sure to place an additional surge protector at each point of use.

If the surge protection device fails, will I know — and will Gulf Power replace it?

The surge suppression device comes with red LED indicator lights that are lit while active. If those lights are out, the surge equipment has been damaged and is not working. If a failed unit is discovered on our routine checks, we will replace it at no charge to you.

Will my business need to be without electrical service to install the device?

Typically, no, but under some circumstances we will need to momentarily cut your power. Before we do, our installers schedule a convenient time for installation with your business.

Do I still need point-of-use surge protection?

Commercial Surge protects motor-driven and resistance-based equipment and their electronic components. In order to protect other electronics like computers, Gulf Power recommends a good, high quality point-of-use surge device.

Is this a lightning protection system?

No, Commercial Surge Protection will not prevent lightning from striking your business. However, Commercial Surge Protection will protect against any lightning related surges and cover any protected motor-driven equipment damaged by a lightning-induced or related surge entering your business through the electric service.

Does the surge suppression equipment protect against single phasing conditions?

No. In order to protect equipment in the event of a single phasing condition, a customer should install special equipment designed specifically for this purpose.
