HVAC Maintenance Leads to Savings

Proper heating, ventilating, and air-conditioning systems (HVAC) are key to maintaining a comfortable, healthy and productive work environment. Since heating and cooling accounts for as much as 30% to 40% of the electricity used in commercial buildings, you can make a big impact on your costs by improving the efficiency of your existing systems or upgrading to a new one.

How Much Can You Save?

Have your system assessed by a CheckMe!® Plus Contractor to see if you qualify for rebates of up to $100 for repairs to optimize your system.

If you're ready to invest in more efficient HVAC cooling equipment, we can help recommend the best solution for your facility. As of Jan. 1, 2016, you may also qualify for incentive of $250 per ton for geothermal systems, up to 75 tons ($18,750) per year.

Note: Incentives are offered on a limited basis, and may vary based on date of purchase and installation. All projects associated with potential incentive payments require pre-authorization by Gulf Power prior to project commencement. All work must be completed and application for incentive must be received by Dec. 31, 2016.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Incentive amounts, program rules, and regulations are subject to change.
  • Qualifying products must be new, and must be purchased and installed during the effective dates of the program.
  • Application must be made within 60 days of the installation of qualifying products.
  • In the event that incentive amounts change, the purchase date of the product or equipment will be used to determine the incentive amount.
  • All projects associated with potential incentive payments require pre-authorization by Gulf Power prior to project commencement.
  • All diagnostic services must be provided by a CheckMe!® Plus Certified Technician of a Participating Contractor and must be completed during the effective dates of the program.

Incentive of $250 per ton, up to 75 tons, will be paid for:

Total system* ground source closed loop geothermal heat pump units meeting the minimum efficiency of 14 EER at 86° (AHRI/IS0-13256-1 application) entering water temperature and a nominal water flow of 3.0 gallons per minute per ton.

Incentive of $125 per ton, up to 75 tons, will be paid for:

Total system* ground source hybrid geothermal heat pump units meeting the minimum efficiency of 14 EER at 86° (AHRI/IS0-13256-1 application) entering water temperature and a nominal water flow of 3.0 gallons per minute per ton.

* Total system defined as a new compressor and evaporator coil capable of providing both cooling and heating

Incentive of $150 per ton, up to 75 tons, will be paid for:

Ground-source closed loop geothermal heat pump units if installed to an existing ground-source closed loop system meeting the minimum efficiency of 14 EER at 86° (AHRI/ISO-13256-1 application) entering water temperature and a nominal water flow of 3.0 gallons per minute per ton.

Prior to payment of incentive, customer must:

  • Submit a completed incentive application
  • Provide documentation of proof of equipment qualifications (including AHRI Certificate of Rating)
  • Provide sales receipt including date of installation
  • Pass Gulf Power's verification process

Note: Neither the payment of an incentive, nor any inspection by Gulf Power shall be deemed as guaranty or warranty.
