Keep Cool with a Cool Roof

Certain roofing materials reflect the sun's heat back to the sky instead of transferring it to the building below. ENERGY STAR-qualified roof products reflect more of the sun's rays and can lower roof surface temperature significantly which means your air conditioner is not working as hard, reducing its workload by 10-15 percent. And you can save with incentives up to $5,000 to businesses that use qualifying roof products.

How Much Can You Save?

As of Jan. 1, 2016, you may qualify for 10 cents per square foot up to $5,000 for reflective roofing installed above air-conditioned space.

Note: Incentives are offered on a limited basis, and may vary based on date of purchase and installation. All projects associated with potential incentive payments require pre-authorization by Gulf Power prior to project commencement.

About Cool Roofs

Cool roof materials reflect and emit the sun's heat back to the sky instead of absorbing it. “Coolness” is measured by two properties, solar reflectance and thermal emittance. The higher the value for each property, the cooler the roof.

Cool roof owners may notice an increase in their heating costs in winter months. This increase is usually greatly outweighed by the cooling energy savings achieved during summer months.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Incentive amounts, program rules, and regulations are subject to change.
  • Qualifying products must be new, and must be purchased and installed during the effective dates of the program.
  • Application must be made within 60 days of the installation of qualifying products.
  • In the event that incentive amounts change, the purchase date of the product or equipment will be used to determine the incentive amount.
  • All projects associated with potential incentive payments require preauthorization by Gulf Power prior to project commencement.
  • Qualifying roofing product must be installed over air-conditioned building space.
  • Built-up or shingle roofs must have a minimum solar reflectance of 73%; light-colored metal roofs must have a minimum solar reflectance of 65%.
  • Prior to payment of incentive, customer must:
    • Submit a completed incentive application.
    • Provide documentation of proof of product qualifications (including manufacturer and the appropriate efficiency specifications of the products).
    • Provide sales receipt including date of installation.
    • Provide a completed and signed W-9 form.
    • Pass Gulf Power's verification process.
  • Neither the payment of an incentive, nor any inspection by Gulf Power shall be deemed as guaranty or warranty.
