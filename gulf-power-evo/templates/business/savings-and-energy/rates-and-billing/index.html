Pricing for Business Customers

We offer two pricing structures for business customers. Read the descriptions below to help determine which one is right for you, based on the nature of your business and the billing history of your business location.

Demand Pricing

Our standard commercial and industrial rates: you get the energy you want, when you want it. Several factors are calculated into demand rates: the amount of power used and how quickly it is used. If yours is a business that has to be open during standard weekday working hours, and uses energy for heating, cooling, and lighting, then a Demand Rate is probably right for you. There are several classes of Demand Rates, depending on your type of business, energy needs and other factors.

Time of Use Pricing

This rate benefits customers with certain load patterns or those who have the flexibility to shift usage to off-peak periods. If your business can operate during the evenings, nights or weekends, or if your business experiences its heaviest energy usage during those times you could realize considerable savings with Time of Use Rates. There are several classes of Time of Use Rates: contact us to discuss the best plan for you.

Billing Services

There never seems to be enough time or enough money, right? These services will make bill-paying faster, and maybe save you some money on your energy bill, too.

Budget Billing

It's easy to plan for your monthly energy bill when you meet certain eligibility requirements. Budget Billing is a free service that averages the highs and lows of your electric bills by giving you an average price to pay each month. We will work with you to create a customized plan using your past energy usage in your current location.

Sign up for Budget Billing

Electronic Funds Transfer

Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) is a free and convenient way to pay your monthly utility bill without worrying about checks, postage or paying on time. You'll receive your regular bill each month and on each bill's due date, the amount you owe will be deducted automatically from your bank account.

To enroll, complete a short form and submit some information about your checking account. Upon verification from your bank, the electronic funds transfer can begin with your next billing.
