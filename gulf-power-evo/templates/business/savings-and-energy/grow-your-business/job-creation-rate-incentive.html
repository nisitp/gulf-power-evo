Supporting Job Creation in Northwest Florida

Gulf Power Company wants to attract business expansion and growth in Northwest Florida. To do our part, we offer a Job Creation Rate Incentive for businesses who relocate here.

We give new qualifying businesses base rate electric price incentives over a four-year period. The potential savings may range from $11,000 for a 200-kilowatt business customer to more than $1.2 million for a 4-megawatt customer. To learn more, and to sign up, see below.

How Much Will My Business Save?

How much businesses save each year through our Job Creation Rate depends on their electricity load size, and which year of the program they're in. There are three incentive electric load categories — 200 to 349 kW, 350 to 999 kW and 1,000 kW or greater.

Year 200 to 349 kW 350 to 999 kW 1,000 kW or Greater
1 20% 40% 60%
2 15% 30% 45%
3 10% 20% 30%
4 5% 10% 15%
5 0% 0% 0%

How to Participate

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for the incentive, participants must hire and maintain the following number of full-time employees within one year of receiving power service at the qualified delivery point:

  • 200 to 349 kW - 10 full-time employees
  • 350 to 999 kW - 25 full-time employees
  • 1,000 kW or greater - 25 full-time employees per 1,000 kW of qualifying load

Participating businesses must:

  • Sign an affidavit verifying that the availability of the incentive is a significant factor in the customer's decision to request service from Gulf Power and must agree to a minimum five-year service agreement.
  • Submit audit documentation verifying the current number of full-time employees (applies only to businesses expanding at an existing facility).
