Energy-Saving Tips

Check out these tips to help you stay comfortable and use your electricity efficiently at the same time.

Lighting

Consider these tips to save money and energy on lighting.

1. Exit signs

Switch to ENERGY STAR® LED models. Exit signs remain on 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, which makes this measure more cost effective.

2. Interior Lighting Controls

Install sensors to control lighting systems where applicable. Determining if daylighting controls, dimmers, occupancy or vacancy sensors are the right strategy for your building is important. Choosing the ideal type of sensor is also essential.

3. Interior lighting

In many cases, upgrading to LED technology makes economic sense, as in recent years, lamp and labor costs to have these installed have dropped significantly.

4. Exterior lighting

Consider replacing outdoor lighting with LEDs. Click here to learn more about our outdoor lighting services.

5. Photocells

Installing automatic controls for exterior lighting has become an industry standard even for existing buildings as installation costs are predominately low.

HVAC

HVAC is at the top of all energy end-use consumption, making it a great place to focus on savings.

1. Programmable thermostats

Installing programmable thermostats to conserve energy by controlling building temperatures is a simple and effective method to save energy.

2. Demand-controlled ventilation

Install demand-controlled ventilation devices on existing ventilation systems. In hot-humid climates, limiting outside air to only what is required could yield great savings.

3. Insulation

Adding ceiling insulation can help lower the heating and cooling costs for your business. Click here to learn more about available incentives.

4. Roofing

Reflect the sun's heat back to the sky instead of transferring it to the building below with ENERGY STAR® qualified roof products. Click here to learn more about available incentives.

5. HVAC repair or upgrade

Make a big impact on your costs by improving the efficiency of your existing systems or upgrading to new equipment with a higher efficiency rating.

Click here to learn more about available incentives or find a CheckMe!® Plus Contractor who can assess your system to see if you qualify for rebates.

Plug Loads

Manage your computers and office appliances efficiently.

1. Office Equipment

Consolidate your Printer, copier, fax, and scanner to an all-in-one ENERGY STAR® model with hibernation mode capabilities.

2. Vending machines

These machines stay on 24/7. Switching to ENERGY STAR® models and using more energy efficient lighting with timers or occupancy sensors can provide substantial savings.

3. Computers

Switch to ENERGY STAR® certified models which are sleep-mode-enabled.

4. Computer Monitors

Switch to ENERGY STAR® certified models which are sleep-mode-enabled.
