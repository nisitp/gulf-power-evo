Get to Know Gulf Power

We are an investor-owned electric utility headquartered in Pensacola, Florida. We serve almost half a million people across Northwest Florida, from the Alabama border, to the Apalachicola River, to the Gulf coast.

The primary focus of our more than 1,300 employees is to serve our customers' energy needs. We are ready to help homeowners and business become more efficient with their energy use, steward the responsible generation of energy and develop new energy sources, and give back to the community we also call home.
