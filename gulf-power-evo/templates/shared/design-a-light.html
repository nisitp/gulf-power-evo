Create Your Own Light

The right streetlamp can really brighten up the area outside of your home or business- literally! Our Create Your Own Light tool allows you to envision the perfect streetlamp for any area. First, select the image that most closely resembles your environment. Then, use the two sets of arrows to scroll through different types of light fixtures and poles to find your perfect match. Once you've selected your favorites, they will be highlighted below; click “Add to Favorites” and fill out the Request More Information form to learn more about purchasing and installing your new light!
